Skule Adventures!

Skule Adventures is a arcade style platformer created by engineering students in the games division of Cloud Club. The game revolves around a first year engineer trying to traverse the UofT campus and make there way to their final exam at the dreaded examination center. From the Sandford Fleming pit to the Lassonde mining building, you'll have to make your way through the perils of first year. The game itself was developed using JavaScript, in particular an object oriented library called Phaser JS. This library allowed for us to quickly test and deploy a working game in no time. There were 3 main components to the development of this game, the front-end assets, the level design, and all the behind the scenes logic that drive the game. The next steps in our game development story is going to be to add online multiplayer co-op!
